A little bit about me...

Hello! Привіт! My name is Danielle Berg and I am just beginning my journey into the world of coding! After working for the library for 18 years, I am looking for a change in careers. One that will mentally challenge me and utilize my love of problem solving and attention to detail! I was introduced to coding by my significant other who had me try out a Learning Javascript course on codecademy which ultimately led me to taking the MIT xPro Full Stack coding boot camp. And here I am! 1/3 of the way through the program!

In my free time, I love spending quality time with my pup Cleo, hiking, traveling, and reading. I also am in the midst of learning Ukranian. Я вчу українську мову. It has taken a backburner to my coding practice, but once boot camp is done I plan on devoting more time to it. My family and friends are very important to me and I regularly spend time with them. I have a tradition of hosting a pumpkin carving party in October and a cookie decorating party in December. I also recently began building legoes with my significant other. We built the 9000+ piece Titanic set, which was epic and took us months!

My dreams and goals for the future...

Being new in the technology field, I realize that I have a lot to learn. So my first goal is to gain experience and to continue to grow my knowledge base. With that goal in mind, I am open to any opportunities that comes my way that will allow me to grow in this field! My ultimate dream is to work remotely, with flexible work hours that would allow me to live my life to the fullest by having the opportunity to travel and hike regularly. I hope to one day work for a company that works in an area that I am passionate about. Currently that would include animals, pet shelters, libraries, books, travel, or the outdoors. Or at the very least, I want to work in a position where I am making a positive impact on the lives of others.
